Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add tasks using natural language?

Simply type or speak your task naturally! For example: "Call mom tomorrow at 3pm" or "Finish report in 2 days". FlowTiq understands dates, times, and priorities automatically.

What is the daily briefing?

The daily briefing provides a personalized overview of your day, including your tasks, schedule, and AI-powered suggestions. Tap the refresh icon to get an AI-enhanced briefing with personalized insights.

How does privacy work?

Your data stays on your device. Tasks, preferences, and personal information are encrypted locally. AI features use secure, privacy-preserving methods. We never see your data.

Can I use FlowTiq offline?

Yes! Most features work offline. The AI briefing requires an internet connection, but task management, scheduling, and focus timer work completely offline.

How do I set up recurring tasks?

When creating or editing a task, enable "Recurrence" and choose daily, weekly, or monthly. You can set specific days, times, and intervals for your recurring tasks.

What are free time suggestions?

When you have free time in your schedule, FlowTiq suggests activities based on your interests (like reading, creative hobbies, etc.). You can customize these in Settings under "Free Time Activities".
