We don't collect your data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

1. Information We Collect

NONE.

FlowTiq is designed with privacy-first architecture. We do not collect, transmit, store, or access any of your personal information, tasks, emails, calendar events, or usage data.

2. Data Storage

100% Local Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your iOS device:

  • Tasks and to-do items
  • Emails (when synced via IMAP)
  • Calendar events (from iOS Calendar)
  • Productivity statistics and focus sessions
  • AI-generated insights and briefings
  • User preferences and settings

Data Location: Your iPhone/iPad only
Data Backup: Not backed up to iCloud
Data Access: Only you, only on your device

3. Data Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted using:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption (military-grade)
  • iOS Keychain for credentials (hardware-backed via Secure Enclave)
  • Biometric protection (Face ID / Touch ID)
