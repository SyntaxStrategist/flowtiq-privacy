FlowTiq Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2025 | Effective Date: November 6, 2025

The Honest Version

We're privacy-first. Your tasks and calendar stay on your device, encrypted. When you use premium AI features, we send only task titles and basic preferences to OpenAI for processing. We never sell your data. Period.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data (Stored on Your Device Only)

FlowTiq stores this data encrypted on your device:

  • Tasks (titles, descriptions, priorities, due dates, recurrence settings)
  • Calendar events (from iOS Calendar)
  • Productivity statistics (tasks completed, focus time)
  • User preferences (name, notification settings, free time activities)
  • Focus session history
  • AI learning data (732+ behavioral data points tracked locally)

Storage: AES-256 encrypted, device-only
Access: Only you, protected by optional biometric lock
Cloud Backup: NOT backed up to iCloud or any cloud service

Cloud Data (Premium AI Features Only)

When you use Premium AI (during 14-day free trial or active subscription), we send:

Sent to OpenAI for Processing:

  • Task titles (e.g., "Finish quarterly report")
  • Task priorities (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
  • Task due dates and times
  • Task types (Regular, Exam, Assignment, Workout)
  • User preferences (name, tone, free time activities - e.g., "Reading", "Creative Hobbies")
  • NOT sent: Task descriptions, notes, personal information beyond preferences

Anonymous Learning (Local Only):

  • Anonymous user ID (random UUID, not linked to you)
  • Behavior patterns (tracked on-device):
    • Task completion rate (e.g., "user completes 80% of tasks")
    • Productive hours (e.g., "user works best at 9-11am")
    • Focus session patterns

Purpose: Improve AI recommendations over time (PersonalizationEngine)
Storage: Encrypted on your device only - never transmitted
Duration: Until you delete the app

2. How We Use Your Data

Local Data (On Your Device):

  • Display your tasks and calendar
  • Generate productivity insights
  • Power focus timer and notifications
  • Never sent to our servers or third parties

OpenAI Processing (Premium AI):

  • Prioritize your tasks intelligently
  • Generate morning briefings
  • Create daily schedules
  • Suggest procrastination interventions
  • Provide personalized free time activity suggestions
  • Processed in real-time, NOT stored by OpenAI
